# 认识菊花

## 简介
秋天来了，爷爷带小女孩认识花园里不怕冷的菊花朋友。

## 旁白

## Page 1
EN: Autumn came, and all the flowers in the garden had faded away.
CN: 秋天来了，花园里的花都谢了。

## Page 2
EN: Only the chrysanthemums were not afraid of the cold, blooming into beautiful flowers.
CN: 只有菊花不怕冷，开出了美丽的花朵。

## Page 3
EN: "Grandpa, what flower is this?" the little girl asked, pointing at the chrysanthemums.
CN: "爷爷，这是什么花呀？"小女孩指着地上的菊花问。

## Page 4
EN: Grandpa smiled and said: "These are chrysanthemums, the flower fairies of autumn."
CN: 爷爷笑着说："这是菊花，是秋天的花仙子。"

## Page 5
EN: The chrysanthemums were yellow, white, and purple, like little suns in the garden.
CN: 菊花有黄色的、白色的、紫色的，像一个个小太阳。

## Page 6
EN: The little girl squatted down and looked closely at the chrysanthemum petals.
CN: 小女孩蹲下来，仔细看着菊花的花瓣。

## Page 7
EN: "Grandpa, the chrysanthemum petals look like noodles!" the little girl laughed.
CN: "爷爷，菊花的花瓣像面条！"小女孩笑了起来。

## Page 8
EN: Grandpa patted her head and said: "Do they look like your little curls?"
CN: 爷爷摸了摸小女孩的头说："它们像不像你的小卷发？"

## Page 9
EN: The little girl touched her own hair and laughed too.
CN: 小女孩摸了摸自己的头发，也笑了起来。

## Page 10
EN: She gently smelled the chrysanthemum, "Ah, how sweet it smells!"
CN: 她轻轻闻了闻菊花，"啊，好香啊！"

## Page 11
EN: Grandpa said: "Chrysanthemums are not only beautiful, but can also be made into tea."
CN: 爷爷说："菊花不仅美丽，还可以泡茶喝呢。"

## Page 12
EN: In the sunset, the little girl and Grandpa looked at the chrysanthemums together and smiled.
CN: 夕阳下，小女孩和爷爷一起看着菊花，笑了。
