# 清明节做青团 - 旁白

## Page 1
EN: Spring came softly, like a whisper on the wind.
CN: 春天轻轻地来了，像风在耳边说悄悄话。

## Page 2
EN: The little rabbit sniffed the air. Something smelled green and sweet.
CN: 小兔子嗅了嗅空气，有一种绿绿的、甜甜的味道。

## Page 3
EN: Mama Rabbit was in the kitchen, her paws covered in something bright and green.
CN: 兔妈妈在厨房里，小爪子上沾满了绿油油的东西。

## Page 4
EN: "It's called mugwort," Mama said. "It grows in the fields every spring."
CN: "这叫艾草，"兔妈妈说，"每年春天田野里都会长出来。"

## Page 5
EN: Together they mixed, and rolled, and shaped little green balls.
CN: 她们一起揉啊揉，搓啊搓，做成了一个个绿色的小球。

## Page 6
EN: Steam rose up, soft and warm. The kitchen smelled like spring itself.
CN: 蒸汽升起来，暖暖的。整个厨房都是春天的味道。

## Page 7
EN: The little rabbit took a bite. Sticky, sweet, and a little bit grassy.
CN: 小兔子咬了一口。黏黏的、甜甜的，还有一点点青草香。

## Page 8
EN: "This is what spring tastes like," the little rabbit said with a smile.
CN: "这就是春天的味道呀。"小兔子笑着说。

## Page 9
EN: And outside, the rain fell gently on the new green grass.
CN: 窗外，春雨轻轻地落在新绿的草地上。

## Page 10
EN: Spring is soft. Spring is green. Spring tastes like qingtuan.
CN: 春天是软软的。春天是绿绿的。春天尝起来像青团。
